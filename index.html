TERMINOLOGY // EPOCH

Unix time is the count of seconds since 00:00:00 UTC on 1 Jan 1970. It serves as a universal standard for tracking time across different computing systems, independent of timezones.

ADVISORY // Y2K38

The Year 2038 problem affects systems storing time as signed 32-bit integers. On Jan 19, 2038, counters will overflow. Critical infrastructure must migrate to 64-bit architectures.
